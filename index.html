Build your Community on Tetra

Tetra is the engine helping you foster communities' trust, identity and brand.

Enabling smarter community

Tetra helps you build and strengthen your community upon an infrastructure, enabling trades and exchange of value.

Coin your tokens of Value

Nowadays people need more than just a payment system: they expect to exchange, share and be rewarded on multi-faceted Values. Tetra makes it possible to build multiple tokens representing those Values.

Open Source Engine

Tetra is an Open Souce Engine. It costs nothing to buy and little to operate, it is customizable and it's easy to plug-in.

Blockchain based

Tetra is based on the Blockchain architecture. Network is distributed, exchanges and transactions are safe. Using blockchain's architecture Cryptodeer is not a central authority.
